About me

A little about who I am

◉
add your photo here

Hi, I'm Kuldeep Gurjar — a robotics engineer and AI researcher specialising in reinforcement learning, motion planning, and intelligent autonomous systems.

I'm currently a Data Scientist (RDE Engineer) at the Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO), Pune, where I work on biped locomotion, dual-arm manipulation, and LLM/VLM integration for defence robots. Before this I was a Senior Research Fellow at DIAT, and a Research Intern at IISc Bangalore's Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems.

My work spans the full stack — from formal methods like Signal Temporal Logic to hands-on hardware deployment on platforms like the Nvidia Jetson AGX Orin, UR10 manipulator, and DRDO's BRS1 biped. I hold an M.Tech in Automation & Robotics from DIAT, Pune.
