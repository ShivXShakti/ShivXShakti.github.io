About me

Who I am

👤 photo.jpg

Hi, I'm Kuldeep Gurjar — a robotics engineer and AI researcher specialising in reinforcement learning, motion planning, and intelligent autonomous systems.

Currently a Data Scientist (RDE Engineer) at DRDO, Pune, working on biped locomotion, dual-arm manipulation, and LLM/VLM integration for defence robots. Previously a Senior Research Fellow at DIAT and Research Intern at IISc Bangalore's Robert Bosch Centre.

My work spans the full stack — from formal methods like Signal Temporal Logic to hands-on hardware on Nvidia Jetson AGX Orin, UR10 manipulators, and DRDO's BRS1 biped. M.Tech in Automation & Robotics from DIAT, Pune.

Dual-Arm Motion Planning
